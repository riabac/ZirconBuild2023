Introduction...


How we live is evolving and modern day expectations are demanding brighter, warmer, more energy efficient homes with flexible, open living spaces. Zircon have been improving and extending homes to the highest standards to meet these expectations in Kings Heath and Moseley since 2008.

We undertake all aspects of domestic building including construction of domestic extensions, loft conversions, garage conversions, internal modifications and structural steelwork, property refurbishment, electrical works, underpinning, roofing, underfloor heating and replacement doors and windows.

Zircon recognises that every homeowner is unique, and each project comes with its own set of challenges. We work closely with you, listening to your needs and preferences, to ensure that the end result not only meets but exceeds your expectations. We take pride in ensuring that your project is completed within the agreed timescales and budget.
